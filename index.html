Who We Are

Ngano Secondary School is a mixed day and boarding, sub-county school located in Ngano, near Ngano Shopping Centre. It is one of the 11 sub-county schools in Gathanji sub-county, Nyandarua county. The school stands out as the school of choice for many students within the region, due to its above average academic performance, exemplary performance in both games and music. An aspiration for all and sundry, here students are shaped to be all-rounded and grounded in character, discipline and academic excellence.

Since its establishment, Ngano Secondary School has grown steadily to become a leading institution in the region. The school is committed to providing quality education and fostering an environment that encourages both academic and personal growth. With a dedicated team of teachers and a supportive community, Ngano Secondary School continues to strive for excellence in all its endeavors.
